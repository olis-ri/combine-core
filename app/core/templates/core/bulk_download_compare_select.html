DPLA Bulk Data Compare

If bulk DPLA data dumps have been downloaded and indexed, it is possible to check the existence of Records against that DPLA data, by looking for matching, unique Item URLs. In DPLA records, this is encoded as the isShownAt field. The requirement is mapping the URL of a Record to a field called dpla_isShownAt to ensure this connection between Combine Records and DPLA Items.
